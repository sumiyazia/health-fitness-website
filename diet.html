Nutrition Guidelines

💧
Hydration Matters

Drink at least 2-3 liters of water daily. Proper hydration boosts metabolism and aids digestion.

🥗
Whole Foods First

Focus on whole, unprocessed foods. They're nutrient-dense and keep you full longer.

⏰
Meal Timing

Eat within an hour post-workout. Timing your meals optimizes recovery and muscle growth.
